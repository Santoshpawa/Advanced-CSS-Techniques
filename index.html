Recipe: Egg Omelette

  • 2 Eggs
  • Spices
    • Corriender Seeds
    • Chopped Ginger
    • Sabji Masala
    • Salt
  • Chopped Capsicum
  • Chopped Onion
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Oil

Add two table spoon of oil and head it up in a low flame for 2 min. Then add corrionder seeds, around small table spoon. Then add capsicum, onion and at last tomotoes gradually after an interval of two minutes. Add spices acording to hyour taste. At last add two eggs in it. Wait for two minutes.
